Design and Manufacturing Pantograph

Back to Projects
Add project images or videos here.

Overview

Designed a pantograph to achieve precise 2× motion scaling, emphasizing stability, lightweight construction, and manufacturability using CNC machining, laser cutting, and waterjet fabrication.

Challenges

  • Maintaining accurate 2× scaling while minimizing weight and cost.
  • Improving stylus stability through tighter tolerances and precision-drilled features.
  • Ensuring smooth motion through iterative design and fabrication adjustments.

Results

  • Consistently produced accurate 2× scaled drawings during testing.
  • Successfully fabricated components using a Wazer waterjet, TRAK K3 CNC knee mill, DoAll vertical band saw, and Trotec 120W laser cutter.
  • Strengthened skills in tolerance control, SolidWorks design, and manufacturing workflows.
