About Me

Jake E. Torres

Mechanical Engineer · Loyola Marymount University

Mechanical Engineering student at Loyola Marymount University (Class of 2027), with strong interests across multiple engineering fields including aerodynamics, design, and applied systems. Aerodynamics Lead of LMU’s Formula SAE team, experienced in CAD, CFD, and project leadership. Proficient in SolidWorks, MATLAB, and GD&T, with proven strengths in teamwork, problem-solving, and technical design.

I split my time between the San Francisco Bay Area and Los Angeles, giving me opportunities to connect with diverse engineering and professional communities. Outside of academics, I love to work out, cook, and explore new challenges, with personal interests in sports, aviation, racing, and hands-on projects. These passions keep me curious, active, and driven to continue growing both inside and outside the classroom.
