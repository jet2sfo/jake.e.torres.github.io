2025

Formula SAE Aerodynamic Side Pods

Overview

Designed and implemented a Formula SAE side pod to balance cooling requirements with aerodynamic performance. The side pod housed radiators and intakes internally while minimizing drag, improving balance, and rear downforce through controlled airflow management.

Challenges

  • Integrating radiators, intakes, and cooling components within a compact side pod while meeting aerodynamic and regulatory constraints.
  • Ensuring side-pod airflow enhanced beam wing and rear diffuser performance.
  • Controlling airflow around and beneath the side pod to reduce drag.

Outcome

  • Achieved a sleek, regulation-compliant design that securely housed all cooling systems.
  • Generated effective downforce, directing high-velocity airflow toward the beam wing and diffuser.
  • Improved efficiency and vehicle stability; passed technical inspection and demonstrated reliable performance in testing and competition.
